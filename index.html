About Me

My passion for tech started with YouTube - watching creators like Mrwhosetheboss, Markus Brownlee, and Linus Tech Tips.

I got hooked on unboxings, tech reviews, PC builds (even though I haven’t built my own yet!), and video games - all of which pulled me deeper into the world of technology.

At that point in time and i fully believed (with just a little refreshing of memory today) i could build a pc for without actually if i was given the tools to do so without help.

When I first started learning coding at university, it wasn’t instant love especially with Java. I found it tough to understand at first, and it made me question if coding was really for me.

But over time, I realised that coding isn’t about being a “know-it-all.” It’s about staying curious, learning through challenges, and improving step by step. That mindset has stuck with me ever since; and today, every project is a new chance for me to learn and grow, with a real passion for front-end design and building great user experiences.

Outside of coding, I'm really into video editing. I grew a 7k+following on TikTok making football edits just using my phone and free tools, pushing my creativity with limited resources along with other pages doing the same. Recently, I've been learning more about long-form editing too and diving deeper into storytelling, pacing, and building better narratives.

Both coding and editing have taught me patience, problem-solving, creativity, and how to turn small ideas into real projects.

I'm constantly learning, improving, and taking action - and I’m excited to keep building and growing.
