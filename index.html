About Me

From Tech Reviews to Building the Web

My passion for tech started with YouTube. Watching creators like Marques Brownlee and Linus Tech Tips hooked me on the world of technology. When I first started coding at university, it wasn’t instant love (especially with Java!), but it taught me a vital lesson: development isn't about knowing everything. It’s about staying curious, solving puzzles, and improving step by step. Today, I have a massive passion for front-end design, UX, and building practical full-stack solutions.

The Storyteller's Edge

Outside of the IDE, I have a strong background in video editing and digital design. Pushing the limits of editing software taught me a lot about pacing, storytelling, and visual flow—skills that translate directly into how I approach front-end user experiences.

Whether I am debugging an API or cutting a video, the core skills are exactly the same: patience, problem-solving, and turning small ideas into engaging user experiences.
