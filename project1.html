Featured Project

PodPlayer
Transcript-Powered AI Podcast Platform

A full-stack podcast web application where the transcript powers every feature — from clickable timestamps to AI-generated summaries — turning passive listening into active, structured engagement.

🔍 The Problem

Podcast listeners often struggle with long-form audio. Finding specific information, maintaining engagement over hours of content, and retaining key takeaways can be a massive friction point for users.

💡 The Solution

PODIQ solves this by integrating AI directly into the playback experience. By offering smart interactions, dynamic summaries, and enhanced UX, it turns passive listening into an active, engaging experience.

⚙️ Tech Stack & Role

Role: Full-Stack Developer & UI/UX Designer
Technologies Used: AI Integration, Front-end Design, Full-Stack Architecture

🚀 Key Features

  • AI-Powered Insights: Dynamically processes audio to provide smart context.
  • Listener Retention Tools: UX designed specifically to keep users engaged for longer sessions.
  • Clean UI/UX: A modern, accessible interface that prioritizes ease of use.
